Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  4. Add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook for 2–3 minutes until softened.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and oregano. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
  9. Spread some ricotta mixture over the noodles, then add meat sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  11. Finish with mozzarella and the remaining Parmesan cheese on top.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
