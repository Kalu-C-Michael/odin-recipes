Jollof Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 6 large tomatoes
  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Mixed vegetables (optional)
  • Cooked chicken or beef for serving

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and set it aside.
  2. Blend the tomatoes, red bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, and half of the onion until smooth.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  4. Slice the remaining half onion and fry it until soft.
  5. Add the tomato paste and fry for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Pour in the blended pepper mixture and cook for about 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the raw tomato taste disappears.
  7. Add the chicken stock, curry powder, thyme, seasoning cubes, salt, black pepper, and bay leaf.
  8. Stir well and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.
  9. Add the rice and stir until evenly coated with the sauce.
  10. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for about 25–30 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.
  11. If the rice is too dry before it becomes soft, add a little water or stock.
  12. Add mixed vegetables if desired and cook for another 5 minutes.
  13. Serve hot with chicken, beef, fried plantains, or salad.
